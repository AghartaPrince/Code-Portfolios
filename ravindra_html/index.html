About me

More about who I am and what I do

 

I work in the public sector in Oxford, UK as a Digital Development Manager. I moved into IT fairly late in my career - you can read my LinkedIn profile to see what I've worked on previously.

I first cut my teeth on web projects in the early 90's when I decided I'd set up a website for the motor racing club that I was involved with. It was all frames and static html pages with a bit of javascript borrowed from other sites.

Since then I have;

  • Led a team to self-build a corporate intranet, learning site building in Drupal 7 alongside Git, Bash and some SQL
  • Further developed this Drupal site to meet new business requirements as they emerge
  • Project managed the redevelopment of a corporate website on a proprietary CMS platform
  • Designed and launched sub-sites using the corporate CMS, but changing PHP templates and styling in CSS
  • Written a Digital Strategy for my employer, and created a sub-site to host it using GitHub Pages

You can view some of my work in my portfolio.

I'm also a keen amateur photographer. You can see more of my photos at my Flickr site.
